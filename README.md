# scistory
SCISTORY is where science and history come alive. 🌍🔬 From the mysteries of black holes to the rise and fall of ancient empires, SCISTORY connects the dots between discoveries of the natural world and the stories of human civilization.

Our mission is to make learning exciting and accessible — blending knowledge with creativity. Whether you’re a curious learner, a student, or just someone who loves exploring ideas, SCISTORY gives you:

    1.📰 Engaging articles on science and history

    2.🧩 Interactive quizzes to test your knowledge

    3.📚 Latest updates in both fields, simplified and fun

At SCISTORY, we believe the past and present of knowledge go hand-in-hand. Together, they inspire the future. 🚀✨
